## ğŸ¯ What's New

### Vision Boards - Multiple Ways to Visualize Your Goals

Create and customize your vision boards in four different styles:

- **Freeform Vision Board** - A Canva-like editor where you can freely place goals, images, and elements
- **Goal Canvas** - Layer-based canvas with customizable backgrounds and goal overlays
- **Physical Board** - Scan or import photos of your physical vision board and add digital goal overlays
- **Grid Board** - Structured tile-based layout perfect for organizing multiple goals

### Habit Tracking & Gamification

- **Daily & Weekly Habits** - Set up habits with flexible scheduling
- **Habit Streaks** - Track your consistency with visual streak indicators
- **Completion Feedback** - Rate your progress and add notes for each completion
- **Location-Based Tracking** - Automatically track habits when you enter specific locations (geofencing)
- **Habit Timer** - Built-in timer for time-based habits

### Task Management

- **Task Checklists** - Break down goals into actionable checklist items
- **Daily Task Tracking** - Mark tasks as complete with per-day completion tracking
- **Due Dates** - Set deadlines for checklist items
- **Completion Feedback** - Add notes and ratings when completing tasks
- **CBT Enhancements** - Optional cognitive behavioral therapy fields for deeper reflection

### Progress & Insights

- **Daily Overview** - See all your habits and tasks in one place
- **Global Insights** - Track progress across all boards with charts and statistics
- **Activity Summaries** - View your completion history and patterns
- **Streak Tracking** - Monitor your consistency over time

### Smart Features

- **Home Screen Widget** - Android widget to quickly view and toggle habit progress
- **Deep Linking** - Quick access to specific goals and habits from widgets
- **Logical Date System** - Track habits based on your personal day cycle, not just calendar days
- **Template Gallery** - Get started quickly with pre-made board templates
- **Board Wizard** - Guided setup to create your first vision board

### User Experience

- **Dark Mode** - Full dark theme support
- **Onboarding** - Interactive carousel to help you get started
- **Journal & Notes** - Rich text editor for journaling and goal notes
- **Settings** - Customize your app experience
- **Multi-Board Support** - Create and manage multiple vision boards
